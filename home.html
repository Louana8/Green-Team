Reuse Repair Recycle
Reusing products is a great way to reduce waste by giving them a new lease of life! This is also a great way to get inventive and express your creativity; you may find yourself taking part in a great new DIY project! For some great ways to get started, have a look at the article here for some inspiration. Additionally, you could try charity shopping and carboot sales to pick up preloved, unique items at a low price. Repairing items allows you to hold onto your favourite things for longer. In terms of textiles, you can even add your own custom touch to your favourite fashions through repairing them with your personal choice of patches, colours and fabrics! Repairing other items also allows you to develop and maintain valuable handyman skills. Did you know that the textiles industry is one of the main polluters today?
