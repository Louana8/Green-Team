Climate change is happening. Much as we may like to we cannot ignore its effects. Increasing temperatures, rising sea levels and more extreme weather events are apparent across the world. These changes will impact on agriculture, health, infrastructure, energy and the environment. Something needs to change. Whilst the biggest and most significant changes undoubtedly need to be made by governments and big business that doesn't mean we can close our eyes to the problem.

We believe that we can all make small changes to help in this fight by:

  • Re-using what we already have
  • Repairing things instead of replacing them
  • Recycling everything that can be recycled

This site is full of tips, advice and links to help people in Sheffield live more sustainably, reduce their impact on the environment and help to contribute to the battle against climate change.
